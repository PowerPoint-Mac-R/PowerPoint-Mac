## Overview

PowerPoint Professional Suite is a complete presentation platform with co-authoring, video export, and enterprise templates for professional presentation production.

Co-authoring enables simultaneous editing by multiple team members with real-time updates and comment-based review workflows.

Video export produces self-running presentations as video files for distribution and posting without requiring PowerPoint for viewing.

Enterprise templates enforce brand standards across organizational presentations with locked design elements and approved asset libraries.

PowerPoint Professional Suite is the presentation platform for organizations that need professional slide creation with the collaboration, branding, and distribution tools that business presentation demands.

## Performance & Integration

PowerPoint is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while PowerPoint processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
